Mobility Masters offers blind, low vision, deaf, hard of hearing, mobility challenged, and other special needs folks the latest gadgets for a more active, independent lifestyle!



Mobility Masters is dedicated to serving the blind, visually impaired, deaf, hard of hearing, mobility challenged, seniors, veterans, and other special needs populations. For decades we have been providing a wide variety of products made for independent, healthy, and active living. We are here to help, to share insights, to bring innovative offerings to our customers, and to show our appreciation for every community we serve!
